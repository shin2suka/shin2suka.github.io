About Me

Bio:

I was "kicked in the ass" by a seven-year old kid when she asked me : if you cut up a pizza seven times, how many piece would you get.” She sneered at my silence and uttered the words: “ aren’t you a math guy?”

I later saw this problem in my third year math class. Just then I realized that knowledge is everywhere and that all it takes is a humble mind to attain. This is the lesson I carry with me through my academic, career and personal life. Whether it is competing in competitions or diving into a small cave; finishing my undergrad at University of Toronto or being a MMF Candidate.

I open my mind towards knowledge and am especially interested in machine learning and finance. Here are my projects and professional experience. I am very enjoyed in 2019 ASA DataFest Competition, where I saw the charming of data science and machine learning in a practical way.
